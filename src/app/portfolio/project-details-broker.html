Broker Management Platform

UX & UI Case Study · Enterprise SaaS Product

Overview

The Broker Management Platform is an enterprise SaaS solution designed to manage large-scale vehicle brokers who lease vehicles to end customers through leasing, renting, or subscription models.

Unlike direct customer leasing, vehicles in this system are owned or leased by brokers. The platform acts as a centralized layer that tracks broker-owned vehicles, customer contracts, financials, and operational data throughout the vehicle lifecycle.

  • Role: Lead UX & UI Designer
  • Platform: Web Application (SaaS)
  • Industry: Automotive / Mobility / Enterprise SaaS
  • Users: Internal Admins, Broker Teams, Finance & Operations
  • Ownership: End-to-End UX & UI

Problem

Managing broker-owned vehicles at scale is operationally complex. Brokers supply vehicles that are further leased or rented to customers, creating multiple layers of ownership, contracts, and financial responsibility.

Existing processes relied on fragmented systems to track brokers, vehicles, customer contracts, invoices, and documentation—making it difficult to maintain accurate records, trace vehicle usage, and manage financial workflows.

Design Goal

Design a centralized broker management system that enables companies to track broker vehicles, customer contracts, financial transactions, and operational history in one scalable, transparent platform.

My Role

  • Collaborated with stakeholders to understand broker-based business models
  • Defined end-to-end workflows for brokers, vehicles, and customer contracts
  • Designed scalable UX patterns for complex enterprise data
  • Created role-based experiences for operations, finance, and admin users
  • Led UI design, usability validation, and iterative refinement

UX Strategy

  • Entity-based design: Clear separation of brokers, vehicles, and customers
  • Lifecycle visibility: Track vehicles from broker onboarding to contract closure
  • Data transparency: Unified view of contracts, invoices, and service history
  • Scalability: Designed for brokers managing large vehicle fleets
  • Consistency: Aligned UX patterns across quotes, orders, and contracts

Key User Flow

  1. Onboard broker and register broker-owned vehicles
  2. Maintain vehicle availability and broker details
  3. Create and manage quotes and proposals
  4. Convert approved proposals into orders
  5. Assign vehicles to customer lease, rent, or subscription contracts
  6. Track contract details, documentation, and invoices
  7. Monitor vehicle service history and funding information

Broker & Vehicle Management

The platform allows internal teams to maintain a complete record of broker profiles and their associated vehicle fleets. Each vehicle is linked to broker ownership while remaining traceable across customer contracts and service history.

Quotes, Orders & Contract Tracking

All customer interactions—from initial quotes and proposals to finalized orders—are tracked within the system. This ensures a consistent audit trail and seamless transition between sales, operations, and finance teams.

Financials, Documentation & History

The system centralizes critical financial and operational data, including invoices, funding details, contractual documents, and vehicle service records.

This enables accurate reporting, compliance readiness, and long-term visibility into broker and customer performance.

Outcome

The Broker Management Platform transformed a complex, multi-party vehicle operation into a structured, scalable system.

By unifying broker data, customer contracts, financials, and vehicle history, the platform reduced operational friction and improved transparency across teams.

Key Learnings

A consistent, role-based UX approach proved essential for managing complexity without overwhelming users.

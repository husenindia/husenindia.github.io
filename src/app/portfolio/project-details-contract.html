Overview

The Contract Management Platform is a generic SaaS solution designed for companies that lease, rent, or subscribe vehicles to customers. The product acts as a centralized system to manage vehicle contracts, terms, invoicing, and contract lifecycle changes between companies and their customers.

Built as a flexible and configurable platform, the solution can be adopted by different organizations regardless of their business size or contract models.

  • Role: Lead UX & UI Designer
  • Platform: Web Application (SaaS)
  • Industry: Automotive / Mobility / Enterprise SaaS
  • Users: Business Admins, Finance Teams, Customers
  • Ownership: End-to-End UX & UI

Problem

Companies managing vehicle leasing, renting, or subscriptions often rely on fragmented tools to handle contracts, invoices, and customer communication. This results in inconsistent contract data, manual invoicing, poor visibility into payment status, and limited flexibility when contract terms change.

Customers also lack a clear, transparent way to view their contract details, track invoices, or request changes during the contract lifecycle.

Design Goal

Design a generic, scalable contract management system that standardizes agreements between companies and customers, supports flexible contract terms, automates invoicing, and simplifies contract lifecycle management.

My Role

  • Worked closely with product owners and stakeholders to define SaaS requirements
  • Designed end-to-end contract creation and management flows
  • Created scalable UX patterns suitable for multi-tenant SaaS products
  • Designed user experiences for both internal teams and end customers
  • Led usability reviews and iterative UI improvements

UX Strategy

  • Generic by design: Configurable to support different business models
  • Lifecycle-focused: Manage contracts from creation to closure
  • Transparency: Clear visibility of terms, invoices, and status
  • Automation: Reduce manual effort through invoice generation
  • Scalability: Designed for multi-company and multi-user environments

Key User Flow

  1. Create a vehicle contract with duration, mileage, and contract type
  2. Assign contract to a customer
  3. Automatically generate monthly invoices
  4. Track paid and pending invoices
  5. Allow customers to view contract and invoice details
  6. Raise contract change requests (mileage, duration, terms)
  7. Approve and update contract changes within the system

Contract & Invoice Management

The platform maintains a complete record of each contract, including agreed terms, vehicle details, billing cycles, and invoice history.

Finance teams can easily monitor unpaid invoices, while customers have transparent access to their payment history and current contract status.

Advanced Features & Add-ons

  • Purchase invoice management for internal financial tracking
  • Formula builder to define mileage and usage-based calculations
  • Configurable contract terms and pricing logic
  • Support for future feature expansion without UX rework

Outcome

The Contract Management Platform unified contract creation, billing, and lifecycle changes into a single, scalable SaaS product.

The solution reduced manual processing for companies, improved invoice tracking accuracy, and delivered a transparent, self-service experience for customers.
